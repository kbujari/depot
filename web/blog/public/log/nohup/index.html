Spawning background processes

Consider the following pattern: Terminal environments with an editor, and a background process reacting to changing files. Before reaching for terminal multiplexers, see if you can get away with simple tty job control. Spawn a background command, still attached to the terminal instance:

program args &

Also redirect output to a file, for when the process writes to the tty from the background:

nohup program args &
