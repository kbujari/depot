Until now (04/11/2024),
I have not truly believed that my thoughts influenced my life,
and I am completely unaware of how it came to be this way.
In some sense,
this is me truly understanding the flaws of my current perception of reality.
This essay **serves as record** to acknowledging this fact,
by will of its very existence.
It feels as though I have put into thoughts,
my blindness to my feelings.
In short,
discomfort should be the natural state,
and it should be aroused whenever it is not active.

Perhaps it was put quite brutally,
but it spend every word carrying the underlying meaning,
and relies on the rest of the paper to describe the application.
